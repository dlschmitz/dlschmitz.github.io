Purpose:

This site being built out for the local Idaho community in the surrounding cities of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. This site will include several key features:

The Storm Center will be dedicated to current weather updates. It will provide a map with weather images and conditions.

The Gallery will focus on some of the wonderful pictures that nature and weather produces. It will include all types of weather pictures that will be available for visitors to download for personal use.

The Town Weather will provide a list of the towns this site covers and the weather for each town. It will also include articles on any events that may be happening within those cities.
