White Space and Clean Design

Apple

Apple.com

I chose the Apple website because they use a lot of white space on their page. While I am not a big Apple fan, I do appreciate and admire how they can successfully make the white space on the page desireable. They also have a very clean look to their images and the flow of the page.

Repitition

Drop Box

Dropbox.com

Dropbox is a site that I have used in the past as a free service. I chose this site because they regularly promote signing up for their services. If we look at the screenshot there are three different ways a person can sign up for their paid service.

Contrast

Netflix

Netflix.com

Netflix offers a very impressive background on the login page. They promote all of the many of the popular movies and shows they currently have available. They also have a strong white font that directs the eyes to signing up or logging in to the site.
